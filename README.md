## 📌 Vision
Agricultural supply chains in India are often plagued by price opacity, middleman exploitation, and a lack of direct market access for farmers. **AgriConnect** is a unified platform designed to democratize the marketplace by providing farmers with direct access to wholesalers and consumers, backed by data-driven insights.

## ✨ Core Features

### 👨‍🌾 For Farmers: Data-Driven Harvests
- **AI Price Forecaster**: Predictive analytics driven by a Node.js model that estimates price trends based on historic market (Mandi) data. Farmers receive a **"Best Day to Sell"** recommendation.
- **Direct Listing**: List crops with quality parameters and expected pricing.
- **Order Management**: Track sales from pick-up to payment.

### 🏢 For Wholesalers: Transparent Sourcing
- **Smart Marketplace**: Browse verified farmer listings, place competitive bids, or opt for an instant "Buy Now" purchase.
- **QR Traceability**: Integrated QR scanner to verify crop origin and quality certificates instantly at the warehouse.
- **Inventory Tracking**: Manage bulk stocks and distribution channels through a dedicated dashboard.

### 🏠 For Consumers: Farm-to-Table
- **Fresh Marketplace**: Buy organic and premium produce directly from farmers.
- **Transparent Pricing**: See exactly who grew your food and where it came from.

## 🛠️ Technical Implementation

- **Frontend**: Built with **React 19** and **Vite** for lightning-fast performance.
- **Styling**: Modern, premium design system using **Vanilla CSS** with a mobile-first responsive approach.
- **Backend Architecture**: **Node.js Serverless Functions** (Vercel) hosting our custom price forecast model.
- **Database & Auth**: Real-time data synchronization and secure authentication powered by **Firebase (Firestore & Auth)**.
- **Tools**: Integrated **Lucide React** for iconography and **Recharts** for market data visualization.
